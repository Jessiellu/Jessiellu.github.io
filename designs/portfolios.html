APP UI Design

2021-06-29

The original intention of the app design is to provide assistance to mothers in raising children during the breastfeeding period.

This is the APP interface design during my school years, responsible for the community and personal community sections. The community section of the app is divided into three parts: Social circle, Hot Topics and Recommended Articles. The interface adopts a simple style, allowing users to focus on the content of the community as much as possible. Therefore, the color of the interface is decorated with theme colors for each section's title, with gray and black as the colors for the content. Enrich the entire image with the cover image of the article and user recorded images of the baby.
